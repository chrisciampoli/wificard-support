Troubleshooting Tips

  • Make sure you've entered the correct WiFi password — the QR code encodes exactly what you type.
  • When scanning, use the default iPhone or Android camera app. Third-party camera apps may not support WiFi QR codes.
  • If sharing or printing isn't working, check that you've granted WiFi Card the necessary permissions in Settings.
  • WiFi Card works entirely offline — no internet connection is needed.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 22, 2026

WiFi Card is designed with your privacy as a core principle. We believe the best privacy policy is the simplest one: we collect nothing.

Data Collection

WiFi Card does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage data or analytics are gathered
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are used
  • No data is sent to any server — the app works 100% offline
  • No third-party SDKs or services are included

Local Storage

WiFi network names and passwords you enter are stored only on your device using local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Photos & Sharing

When you save a card to your photo library or share it, that action uses your device's built-in capabilities. We have no access to your photos, contacts, or shared content.

Children's Privacy

WiFi Card does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.
